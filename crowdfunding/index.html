Crowdfunding Issue Four

I can hardly believe Lila Jean Vintage has reached its fourth issue. It's been a long journey and we still have a long way to go but it's important to celebrate the milestones and for us, reaching Issue Four is one of them.

We decided to make issue four the International Issue, focusing on travel and all things vintage abroad. To really set this issue apart I decided I wanted to expand our writers and include more voices in the magazine, I also have an idea that involves some illustrations, and of course we intend to pay all our freelancers – being a freelance writer myself I feel very strongly about this. It's our intention to commission writers both here and abroad so after much consideration we’ve decided to enlist the help of crowdfunding to fund our fourth issue. Money raised will pay all our amazing freelancers, including design, as well as ensuring we can continue to print the magazine at the same quality and locally within Australia. Again, supporting small business is something we feel very strongly about.

We already ave some great content inluding an exclusive interview with Burlesque star Dita Von Teese ahead of her visit to Australia, an interview with vintage maverick Doris Raymond of The Way We Wore in L.A., we look back at Qantas' 95 years in the sky and the top 10 vintage films to make you want to travel.

So we need your help. We have some great rewards on offer thanks to our friends at Trésors, Audrey's Wardrobe, Evelyn Wood Vintage Fashion House and The Gold Hatted Lovers. If you can't donate you can still help out by simply sharing the campaign - spreading the word is half the challenge!

Thank you for your support. I can't wait to show you what the fourth issue has in store!
