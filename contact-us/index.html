Want to be in Lila Jean?

We welcome collaborations with writers, photographers, illustrators and other creatives who have an idea they'd love to share. Part of what makes Lila Jean Vintage different from other publications is its desire to collect and collate anecdotes and stories from its readers. From photographs of your grandparents to tales of your parents in the swinging 60s - we want to know your story - and these will contribute to a growing social archive. Regular call outs for content are made through our social channels, the website and in the magazine itself, as well as for our regular sections: I remember when, Vintage Finds and This old thing. If you would like to be involved with Lila Jean drop us a line at submissions@lilajeanvintage.com and don't hesitate to get in touch if you have any questions or would like more information.

Submission terms and conditions

All material submitted to Lila Jean Vintage is subject to editing. Unfortunately we cannot guarantee all submissions will be published. Preferred method of submissions is via email. Postal submission must be organised with the Editor prior to submission. Scanned images must be at least 300 dpi. Submitted content cannot be reproduced without the permission of the publisher.
