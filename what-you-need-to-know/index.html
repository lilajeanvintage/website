Privacy Policy

Lila Jean Vintage are committed to maintaining and using all personal information responsibly. By providing us with your information you are accepting the regulations outlined in this privacy policy.

We collect information when you place an order through our online store, subscribe to our newsletter, participate in any competitions or giveaways or join our social networking sites.

The information collected when you place an order is used to process the order or alert you to any issues. You will be added to our monthly newsletter which you can unsubscribe from at any time. Your personal information will never be disclosed to a third party or used for any other purpose than that which it was given.
